# Smart Shipping

Select a Sales row and choose **FlipTracker Pro → Smart Shipping Assistant**. Enter packaged item dimensions and weight. The assistant searches active box inventory, recommends the smallest fitting box, applies the lowest or selected editable carrier estimate, and saves the result to the sale.

Rates in Shipping Settings are planning estimates, not live carrier quotes. Replace them with your negotiated or typical rates. Box dimensions must appear in the Packaging Size field as `L x W x H`, such as `30 x 20 x 15 cm`.
