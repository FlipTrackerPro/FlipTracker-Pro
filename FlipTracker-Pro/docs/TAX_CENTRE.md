# CRA Tax Centre

The Tax Centre uses the tax year stored in `Settings`. It summarizes sales, item cost, expenses, inventory, GST/HST, and mileage estimates.

## Important limitations

- It is a bookkeeping aid, not tax filing software.
- The GST/HST estimate follows the regular-method concept; Quick Method users need professional adjustments.
- Review ITC eligibility and business-use percentages.
- Inventory valuation must be applied consistently and may require fair-market-value adjustments.
- Mileage-rate estimates do not replace actual motor-vehicle expense calculations where applicable.
