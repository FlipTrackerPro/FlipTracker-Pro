# Architecture

- Main.gs: installer and schema migrations
- Config.gs: constants and headers
- Menu.gs: spreadsheet menu
- Utilities.gs: helpers
- AdminSettings.gs: lists and settings
- Inventory modules: sourcing workflow
- Sales.gs: realized sales and profit
- Expenses.gs: operating expenses
- Mileage.gs: business travel
- Packaging.gs: supply inventory
- Dashboard.gs: KPIs

Upgrade functions must preserve user-entered data.
